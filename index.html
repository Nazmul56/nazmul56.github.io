About me.

I'm a Bangladeshi student, passionate about IT, Programming, Android and technology in general.
I love Open Source: in my free time I like to code and design some interesting stuff that I usually release on GitHub.

In June 2013 I became an Ubuntu Member and I joined the Inovation Project, a little project to improve Ubuntu and Linux's design.
Since summer 2014 I learned to code in Java and JavaScript and I became very interested in Android's development. I started to make Android apps, with a great success (2000.000+ downloads on Google Play right now). I've also worked on a Calculator, a Torch, and Hermes, an IRC client for Android made with material design in mind.
Our little project keep growing and growing and in 2015 we decided to register a Private Limited Company in UK. Now Inovation Project Ltd. has 5 members, working to develop new innovative apps for Android and improve Linux's user experience.
